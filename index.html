About

Yifan Hu is a graduate student in Applied Mathematics at University of California, Santa Cruz.

His research interests include:

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • High-order numerical methods for Partial Differential Equations (PDE).
  • Shock-capturing methods for Magnetohydrodynamics (MHD)
  • High Performance Computing (HPC)
  • Hardware-specific optimization for numerical methods implemented on graphics processing unit (GPU)
